Workflow

The current Trending Words to use in Youtube Video Titles

Steps:
1. Get the trending videos on Youtube
2. Get all title names, and take every word from every title except for prepositions and symbols
3. Query each word with the Youtube search function, resulting in videos found by Youtube with that word being the keyword to these videos
4. Get the average view count of the first 10 videos resulted from this word.
5. Find the word with the highest average view count when queried on youtube's search function
